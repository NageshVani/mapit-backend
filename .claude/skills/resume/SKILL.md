   - 🔜 **Up Next** (ordered list of next steps from the file)
   - ⚠️ **Blockers / Watch-outs** (any open issues or constraints I must not forget)
3. Ask: *"Ready to continue — should I start with [first item in Next Steps], or do you want to adjust the plan?"*

Do NOT begin any coding or file changes until I confirm the direction.
Keep the summary tight — it's a briefing, not a transcript.
