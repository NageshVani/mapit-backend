❓ How MapIt Works

  • 🗺 Browse: listings near you appear as pins on the map (or the list, on mobile) — tap a pin to view details, filter by category and distance in the sidebar
  • ➕ Post: tap Post a Listing in the header to list a home, vehicle, or item
  • ❤ Save: tap the heart icon on any listing to save it — find it later in the Saved tab
  • 🤝 Contact a seller: open a listing and tap I'm Interested to send them a message
  • 🗑 Delete a listing: open My Ads, select your listing, then use the delete option
  • 📍 Change your location: use My Locations in the profile menu
